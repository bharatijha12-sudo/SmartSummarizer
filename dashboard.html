Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this website?

Any student from CBSE, ICSE, State Board or other boards who wants to save time and revise faster.

Does it work with handwritten notes?

Handwritten notes may work if the handwriting is very clear, but typed text gives much better results.

Will this replace my textbook?

No. This website is for revision and quick understanding. You should always read your textbook properly at least once.

Can I use it during exams?

You can use it while preparing, but follow your school and exam rules. This tool is made for learning, not cheating.

Is my data saved permanently?

Your basic details are stored in your browser to personalise your experience. You can clear browser data to remove it.

What is the best way to use this tool?

First, read the chapter once. Then use this tool to convert it into summaries and revise using those summaries + tips & tricks.
